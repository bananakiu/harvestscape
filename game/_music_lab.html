— original reference vibes, for comparison —

Minecraft · C418

Sparse, weightless, faintly melancholic. Soft piano and pads with lots of air.

Stardew Valley · ConcernedApe

Warm, folky, hummable. Marimba and pluck with a real tune and a spring in its step.

RuneScape · old-school

Modal, medieval-fantasy. Harp, flute and lute — one wandering, one jaunty.

Each track is ~a dozen lines of the same primitives the game already has (note() → osc + envelope + reverb/delay). Porting is mechanical once you choose.
